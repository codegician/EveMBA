Active Lesson MODULE 3 / 7
Arbitrage & Price Discovery
Arbitrage is the simultaneous purchase and sale of an asset to profit from price differences in different markets. In finance, true arbitrage is "risk-free" — but in practice, execution risk, transport cost, and information lag all introduce risk.

In EVE Frontier, when Tritanium prices diverge between Sector 7 and Sector 12, you have an arbitrage window. Your gate infrastructure is your execution mechanism — lower transit time = lower execution risk.
ARBITRAGE PRICE DISCOVERY MARKET MICROSTRUCTURE
🚀 Your EVE Frontier application: Current spread between Sector 7 and 12 is ~18% on mineral transit. With 3 gate hops and ~12 min travel, your net arbitrage yield is approximately 11–14% after fuel costs. That's a strong risk-adjusted return if you can lock the route.
